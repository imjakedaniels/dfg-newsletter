INTRODUCING a new quarterly feature for anyone looking to get more out of living around Toronto! 

Yep, we’ve launched a newsletter to highlight worthwhile data events, community resources, and self-improvement tips straight to your digital mailbox.

Oh wait, where are my manners… you’re probably wondering:

"How did this make its way to my handy inbox???" 

The reason is: you’ve signed up for one of our events before. 

We’ve hosted datathons and discussions in the past that you’ve showed interest or participated in – sound familiar?

We wanted to reciprocate the support you’ve given us by sharing upcoming events, recaps, and healthy discussions on the areas that really make humans tick: our bodies, minds, community, and careers.

Tall order? Sure, but we're gonna give it a shot. 

And hopefully, we can turn this digital salutation into a real one.
